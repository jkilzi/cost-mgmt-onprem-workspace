# Raw sources (immutable)

Drop **source** material here for **ingest** into the wiki (articles, exports, notes). Agents **read** these files and **integrate** summaries and links into pages under `wiki/` — they **do not edit** files in `wiki/raw/`.

After ingest, add or update summary pages in the wiki, refresh [`../index.md`](../index.md), and append [`../log.md`](../log.md).
